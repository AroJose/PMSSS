About PMSS

The PMSS Scholarship Portal is designed to streamline the application, verification, and disbursement process for scholarships. Our goal is to make education accessible and support students in their academic journey. PMSS aims to provide financial aid to deserving students, ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder their educational aspirations.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Indian citizen
  • Enrolled in a recognized institution
  • Meet the income criteria set by the scholarship
  • Good academic standing
  • Applicants must have a valid Aadhaar number
